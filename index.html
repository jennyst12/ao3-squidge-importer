ao3-squidge-importer

Bookmarklet to import a fanfic from AO3 into SquidgeWorldArchive. Please only use on your own fics or those you are authorised to archive, and pay attention to the terms of both archives for what types of works are allowed - there are slight differences.

Created with help from Claude AI.

  • Add both bookmarklet files to your bookmarks.
  • Make sure you're logged in to both AO3 and Squidge.
  • Open a fic on AO3 in whole work view in your browser.
  • Click first bookmarklet. It will copy the details of your fic and then navigate you to Squidge's new work form.
  • Make sure the page has fully loaded.
  • Click second bookmarklet. It will fill in all the fields it can. It will backdate the work to the original AO3 posting date.
  • Go through the tags, check and edit, select from the autocomplete where relevant, and hit enter at the end of each field to make them save.
  • Post your work.
